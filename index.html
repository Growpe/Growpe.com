Welcome to GrowPe

GrowPe is a next-generation UPI-based digital wallet designed exclusively for teenagers and minors. We provide a safe, secure, and intelligent payment ecosystem that allows young users to manage their expenses while giving full control to their parents. GrowPe comes equipped with powerful parental supervision tools, customizable spending limits, real-time transaction tracking, and education-first design that promotes digital finance literacy among youth.

With the rise in digital payments across India, it's important to include the younger generation in a way that is both secure and educational. GrowPe is built with the mission to empower India's youth and give families the confidence to go cashless. Whether it’s paying at a shop, sending money to a friend, or learning how to save, GrowPe is the perfect app for the future of digital India.

We believe financial literacy should start early. That’s why GrowPe is not just an app, it's a movement — to raise financially aware, responsible, and smart future citizens.
